STAGE OVERVIEW

Bubble Man's stage is an underwater gauntlet filled with spikes, jellyfish-like enemies, and fish that shoot smaller robots at you. Water changes Mega Man's jump height, so you can leap much higher than usual, but you also have to be careful not to touch the ceiling spikes.

ABOUT BUBBLE MAN

Bubble Man is one of Dr. Wily's Robot Masters in Mega Man 2, designed for underwater combat. Defeating him gives you the Bubble Lead, a slow-moving bubble that rolls along the ground and can also travel across water surfaces, making it perfect for scouting for hidden pits and damaging enemies low to the ground.

TIPS AND WEAKNESSES

In the fight, Bubble Man swims around the room and fires Bubble Lead shots while small bubbles rise from the floor, trying to push you into the spike ceiling. The biggest danger is accidentally jumping too high and impaling yourself, so keep your jumps short and controlled.

Bubble Man is very weak to the Metal Blade, which can hit him easily from below or from a distance. Aim Metal Blades diagonally upward while standing under him, and continue firing as he rises and falls, minimizing your own jumping so you don't brush against the spikes at the top of the room.
