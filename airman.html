STAGE OVERVIEW

Air Man's stage is built high in the sky, with floating platforms, cloud enemies, and strong winds that try to push Mega Man off the ledges. Many sections require precise jumps between moving platforms while dodging projectiles from airborne foes.

ABOUT AIR MAN

Air Man is a Robot Master with a massive fan built into his torso, which he uses to generate powerful gusts of wind and tornadoes. Defeating him grants you the Air Shooter, a weapon that fires three small tornadoes upward at an angle, great for hitting enemies above or in tight spaces.

TIPS AND WEAKNESSES

In the boss fight, Air Man stands in place and fills the screen with patterns of tornado projectiles while gusts of wind push you backward. The trick is to learn the gaps in each tornado pattern and move in close between volleys so you can land shots.

Air Man is weak to the Leaf Shield, which can tear through his health quickly once you get close enough to hit him. Time your approach so you walk into him right after a tornado pattern ends, then release the Leaf Shield to deal big damage before retreating to avoid the next wave.
