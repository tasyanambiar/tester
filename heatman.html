STAGE OVERVIEW

Heat Man's stage runs through a lava-filled facility with disappearing and reappearing blocks over instant-death pits. One of the most infamous sections requires you to memorize or react quickly to a long sequence of vanishing blocks, or use items to skip the most dangerous parts.

ABOUT HEAT MAN

Heat Man is designed like a living lighter, with a body that can close up into a protective box before launching fiery attacks. When you defeat him, you obtain the Atomic Fire, a chargeable weapon that can be powered up to unleash extremely powerful fire shots.

TIPS AND WEAKNESSES

In battle, Heat Man often encloses himself in a fiery box and rockets across the room trying to hit Mega Man, then stops and fires fireballs when he reappears. If you keep a mid-range distance, you can dodge his charges by jumping or sliding just as he rushes by.

Heat Man is weak to Bubble Lead, which can take off large chunks of his health with only a few hits. Fire Bubble Lead at him as soon as he appears from his box form, then quickly get ready to move as he charges again, repeating the pattern until he goes down.
