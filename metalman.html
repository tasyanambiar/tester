STAGE OVERVIEW

Metal Man's stage is set inside a giant factory full of spinning gears and moving conveyor belts. The belts will push Mega Man left or right depending on the arrows, so you have to time your jumps carefully or you will slide into enemies or pits.

ABOUT METAL MAN

Metal Man is one of Dr. Wily's Robot Masters in Mega Man 2. When you defeat him you earn the Metal Blade, one of the strongest weapons in the game because it can shoot in eight directions and uses very little weapon energy.

TIPS AND WEAKNESSES

In the first fight you can beat Metal Man just using the Mega Buster if you learn his jumping and blade pattern. Watch the conveyor belt on the floor because the direction changes during the fight, which can push you into his Metal Blades if you are not careful.

Metal Man is weak to the Quick Boomerang, and in later rematches he is extra weak to his own Metal Blade, which can defeat him in only a few hits. Jump over the low blades, let the high ones fly over your head, and fire whenever he lands.
