STAGE OVERVIEW

Wood Man's stage takes place in a dense, mechanical forest full of robotic animals like bats, wolves, and giant fire-breathing dogs. The level mixes tight corridors with open areas and rain-like leaf projectiles, forcing you to manage enemy waves and environmental hazards together.

ABOUT WOOD MAN

Wood Man is a Robot Master whose body is largely constructed from wood, with a metal core underneath for protection. When you defeat him, you earn the Leaf Shield, a defensive weapon that forms a ring of leaves around Mega Man, which can then be fired forward as a projectile.

TIPS AND WEAKNESSES

During the fight, Wood Man periodically raises the Leaf Shield to make himself invulnerable and launches waves of leaves that fall in a set pattern before sending the shield toward you. The key is to recognize the rhythm: jump over or between the falling leaves, then attack during the brief moments when his shield is gone.

Wood Man takes massive damage from a fully charged Atomic Fire, which can remove most of his health in a single hit. Metal Blade is also very effective and easier to use, letting you damage him from a safer distance, especially while he's between Leaf Shield cycles.
