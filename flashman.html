STAGE OVERVIEW

Flash Man's stage is among the shorter ones in Mega Man 2, taking place in a blue, high-tech facility with slippery floors and branching paths. The main hazards are the ice-like surfaces that make it easy to slide into enemies or off ledges if you are not careful.

ABOUT FLASH MAN

Flash Man is one of Dr. Wily's Robot Masters in Mega Man 2. When you defeat him, you earn the Time Stopper, a special weapon that freezes time and can completely halt enemies and stage hazards for a limited duration.

TIPS AND WEAKNESSES

In the boss fight, Flash Man runs and jumps around the room and occasionally activates the Time Stopper to freeze Mega Man in place while he fires his buster. Staying on the platforms and keeping your distance makes it easier to avoid getting pinned in a corner.

Flash Man is weak to the Metal Blade, which can hit him easily from many angles and does strong damage. You can also use Crash Bombs, but Metal Blade is more practical thanks to its low energy cost and eight-direction firing.
