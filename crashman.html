STAGE OVERVIEW

Crash Man's stage is a tall construction-like tower filled with ladders, moving platforms, and enemies that try to knock you off narrow ledges. Parts of the level force you to climb while dodging projectiles from above and below, making positioning and timing critical.

ABOUT CRASH MAN

Crash Man specializes in explosive weaponry and mobility, leaping around while firing Crash Bombs that stick to surfaces and detonate after a short delay. Defeating him rewards you with the Crash Bomber, a powerful weapon used to destroy certain walls and bosses in Wily's fortress.

TIPS AND WEAKNESSES

Crash Man jumps frequently and often tries to land near Mega Man before firing a Crash Bomb, which can make the fight chaotic in close quarters. Try to control the vertical space by using the platforms so you have time to react to his jumps.

Crash Man is weak to the Air Shooter, which can hit him multiple times if all three tornadoes connect while he is jumping. Lure him into predictable jumps, then fire Air Shooter as he rises or falls so the tornadoes catch him for heavy damage.
